Use Case Name
DeleteInformation
Participating Actors
Started by Employee
Event Flow.
1. Employee requests the deletion of a news item.
2. The system displays the list of existing news items
3. The employee chooses a news item. 4. The system displays a form the
confirmation of the operation. 5. The employee confirms the operation
through the form. 6. The system receives the form, and deletes the data
of the news from the system
Entry condition
This case requires that the employee has gone through the authentication procedure.
Exit condition
The system has performed the deletion of the news, OR
If an error occurred in the deletion request, the system calls the use case ServiceNotAvailable
Quality RequirementsNot Available.
