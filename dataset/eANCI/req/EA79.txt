Use Case Name
ViewInformation
Participating Actors
Started by Citizen
Flow of events
1.
The Citizen requests to view the information in the area.
2. The system returns information regarding the territory by displaying data such as:
- libraries; - events; - museums; - promotions present in the territory.
Entry condition
This case requires that the citizen has logged into the system.
Exit condition
The Citizen has viewed the information, OR
In case the system is not available it invokes the use case ServiceNotAvailable.
Quality RequirementsNot Available.
