Use case name
identityCardRenewal
Participating Actors
Started by Citizen, continued employed
Event Flow
1. Citizen presses on the Renew ID Card button.
2. The system directly sends a renewal request from the Citizen to the appropriate clerk.
3. The Citizen goes to the counter to renew the ID card.
Condition of Entry
The Citizen must be authenticated in the system.
Exit condition
➢ The System creates the document to be delivered to the appropriate office, OR ➢ In the case where the service is not
available the use case ServiceNotAvailable is invoked.
Quality Requirements Not Provided.
