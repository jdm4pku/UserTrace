Use Case Name
RequestElectricalCase
Participating Actors
Started by Citizen, continued by employee
Flow of events
1. The Citizen presses on the Electoral Card button.
2. The system displays the card request form.
The Citizen confirms the operation 4. The system sends a notification
to the clerk. 5. The citizen will receive the election card which
Will be delivered to his/her home address.
Condition of entry
The Citizen must be authenticated in the system.
The voter card has not been sent.
Exit condition
➢ The System alerts the clerk, OR ➢ In the case where the service is not available the case is invoked
of use ServiceNotAvailable.
Quality Requirements Not Provided.
