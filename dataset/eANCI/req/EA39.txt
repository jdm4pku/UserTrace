Use case name
LostElectricCard
Participating Actors
Started by Citizen, continued by employee
Flow of events
1. The Citizen presses on the Lost Electoral Card button.
2. The system directly sends a lost card notification to the appropriate clerk.
3. The Citizen goes to the clerk's office with a copy of the lost card report.
Condition of entry
The Citizen must be authenticated in the system.
Exit condition
➢ The System creates the document to be delivered to the appropriate office, OR ➢ In the case where the service is not
available the use case ServiceNotAvailable is invoked.
Quality Requirements Not Provided.
