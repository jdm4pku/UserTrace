Use case name
ViewReservationConsultation
Participating actors
Started by Citizen
Flow of events
1.
Citizen requests to view the reservation made at a consultation center.
2. The system returns information regarding the selected reservation by displaying data such as:
- Date and time; - Consultatory.
Entry condition
This case requires that the Citizen has carried out the authentication procedure.
Exit condition
The Citizen has viewed the information about the reservation, OR.
In case the citizen wants to modify/delete a reservation, the system calls the use case, ModifyPreservationIntercourse/Elimi nationPreservationIntercourse .
